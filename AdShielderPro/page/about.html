Ad Shielder Pro - browser extension for blocking advertisements.

Ad Shielder Pro - is your reliable ally in ensuring your comfort in the online world. This application is designed to effectively block annoying advertisements on web pages, allowing you to enjoy content without unnecessary distractions. Accelerated page loading, traffic optimization, and protection against malicious elements make AdBlocker Pro an indispensable tool for uninterrupted and secure online experiences.

These are commands for managing active tabs in the browser

  • Handle clicks in the extension menu
  • Pin/Unpin the current tab (Ctrl+Shift+Up)
  • Duplicate the current tab (Ctrl+Shift+Down)
  • Move the current tab to the beginning (Ctrl+Shift+Left)
  • Move the current tab to the last (Ctrl+Shift+Right)
